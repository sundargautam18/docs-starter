## Artwork Upload Process

Uploading an artwork consists of **4 simple steps**. Complete each section carefully to help collectors discover your artwork and make informed purchasing decisions.

Once you're familiar with the dashboard, you're ready to upload your first artwork.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1 — Basic Information">
    Begin by clicking **Add Artwork** from your Artist Dashboard.

    On this page, you will provide the basic information about your artwork, including:

    - Upload the primary artwork image
    - Select whether the artwork is available for sale
    - Artwork title
    - Artwork description
    - Listed price
    - Enable or disable **Make an Offer**
    - Date of creation

    > **Make an Offer**
    >
    > By enabling this option, collectors can submit purchase offers below the listed price. Offers are typically expected to be within a reasonable range of the listed price.

    <Frame>
      ![Part1 Updated](/images/part1-updated.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2 — Artwork Details">
    The second page helps collectors discover your artwork through search and filtering.

    Select the options that best describe your artwork:

    - Dominant color(s)
    - Artwork category
    - Subject
    - Style
    - Medium
    - Material
    - Tags

    These selections improve search results and help buyers find artworks that match their preferences.

    <Frame>
      ![Part 2 Updated](/images/Part-2-updated.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3 — Additional Information">
    Provide additional details about your artwork.

    ### Artwork Information

    - Multi-panel composition
    - Hanging hardware included
    - Frame included
    - Packaging method

    ### Dimensions

    Enter the artwork dimensions in:

    - Centimeters (cm)
    - Inches (in)

    ### Shipping Information

    Provide:

    - Artwork weight
    - Artwork pickup location

    These details help us calculate shipping costs and prepare your artwork for delivery.

    <Frame>
      ![Part 3 Updated](/images/part-3-updated.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4 — Background Image & Publish">
    Upload a background image for your artwork.

    This image is used as the **Virtual Wall Preview**, allowing collectors to visualize how your artwork may look in an interior setting.

    Once everything has been reviewed:

    - Verify all artwork information
    - Check pricing
    - Confirm dimensions
    - Click **Publish Artwork**

    Your artwork will then become available for collectors on Annapurna Art.

    <Frame>
      ![Part 4 Updated](/images/part-4-updated.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Before You Publish

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Use High-Quality Images" icon="image">
    Upload clear, well-lit images that accurately represent your artwork. You may upload **up to 5 images** showing different angles, textures, framing, and close-up details.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Review Before Publishing" icon="check-check">
    Double-check your title, pricing, dimensions, category selections, and shipping information before publishing. Accurate information helps collectors make confident purchasing decisions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
