#### About Us

Annapurna Art is proud to offer both physical and virtual exhibitions for artists and buyers. Physical exhibitions are held in our gallery space, while virtual exhibitions are hosted online.

Both types of exhibitions offer a unique opportunity for artists to showcase their work and for buyers to discover new art. Physical exhibitions allow for a more immersive and personal experience, while Virtual exhibitions offer a wider reach and greater convenience.
