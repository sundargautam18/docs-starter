#### How to sell arts?

Follow the simple steps and start selling your arts in future.

![steps](https://annapurnaart.com/_next/image?url=%2Fhelpicon%2Fstep1.png&w=640&q=75)

#### Sign Up and Account Approval

To start the process, artists need to sign up on Annapurna Art and provide the necessary personal and financial documents for verification. After their account gets approved, artists can access their artist dashboard, where they can manage their art-related activities.

![steps](https://annapurnaart.com/_next/image?url=%2Fhelpicon%2Fstep2.png&w=640&q=75)

#### Initiate Exhibition

Within the artist dashboard, artists will find an option labeled "Exhibition" in the left menu. Clicking on this option allows artists to initiate the creation of a new exhibition. This is where they can showcase their artwork to a wider audience.

![steps](https://annapurnaart.com/_next/image?url=%2Fhelpicon%2Fstep3.png&w=640&q=75)

#### Select Artwork and Customize

During the exhibition setup, artists are required to select a minimum of 5 artworks that they wish to feature. Once the artworks are chosen, artists are directed to a playground-like interface. Here, they have the creative freedom to customize various aspects such as colors and strokes to match their artistic vision.

![steps](https://annapurnaart.com/_next/image?url=%2Fhelpicon%2Fstep4.png&w=640&q=75)

#### Confirm and Publish

Once satisfied with the customization, artists can proceed to confirm the setup. This step serves as a final review of the virtual exhibition's appearance and features. With the confirmation, the virtual exhibition becomes ready for publishing on Annapurna Art, allowing artists to share their art with the world.
