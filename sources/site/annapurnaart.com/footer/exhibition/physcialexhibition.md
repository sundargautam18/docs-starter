#### Physical Exhibition

Physical exhibitions are a great way for artists to showcase their work and for buyers to discover new art. They offer a unique opportunity for people to experience art in person, which can be a more engaging and impactful experience than viewing art online. At Annapurna Art, we host physical exhibitions on an occasional basis. We believe that physical exhibitions are an important part of the art world, and we are committed to providing artists with a platform to showcase their work.

![how to sell image ](https://annapurnaart.com/_next/image?url=%2Fhelpicon%2Fhowtosell.png&w=3840&q=75)

#### Participating in a Physical Exhibition

We will select the artworks out of available artworks in our site. An artist will get notified to it and if he/she is okay with it, we will display for the exhibition.

Viewers are allowed to scan a QR code to reveal the hidden artwork on their devices, offering an exclusive digital experience. The physical exhibition is only accessible to local buyers and viewers due to the concealed nature of the artworks, which require a QR code to access.

![steps](https://annapurnaart.com/_next/image?url=%2Fhelpicon%2Fstep1.png&w=640&q=75)

#### Selection of Artworks

Our curator looks over the available artworks present on our websites and selects the most captivating artwork that will be perfect to exhibit. If your artwork will be shortlisted, we promptly ask for the permission to you.

![steps](https://annapurnaart.com/_next/image?url=%2Fhelpicon%2Fstep2.png&w=640&q=75)

#### Artwork Delivery & Display

Once the artworks have been selected, the gallery will contact the artists to arrange for their delivery of the artworks. The artworks will be displayed in the gallery space for a specified period of time.

![steps](https://annapurnaart.com/_next/image?url=%2Fhelpicon%2Fstep3.png&w=640&q=75)

#### Hidden QR Art Sale

During this time, the artworks will be available for purchase by buyers. However, there is a twist: the paintings will be hidden from public view. The viewer will need to scan a QR code to access the paintings. Once the QR code is scanned, the painting will be revealed on the viewers device

![steps](https://annapurnaart.com/_next/image?url=%2Fhelpicon%2Fstep4.png&w=640&q=75)

#### Exclusive Exhibition Access

The physical exhibitions are only open to buyers and viewers who are in the same country as the gallery. This is because the paintings are hidden from public view and can only be accessed by scanning a QR code.
